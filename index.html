My Artist Statement

Eyes,Video Art,2018

The title of my project is Eyes. I am envisioning this piece to be presented in a gallery installation. My concept for this project was to screen record my usage of social media and give the feeling of surveillance.

E-Sports,Installation with Video Art,2018

This piece was inspired by my love video games and how it has brought many people together. The main concept of this piece was to show different perspectives of what goes on in an E-sport event, or competitive gaming.
